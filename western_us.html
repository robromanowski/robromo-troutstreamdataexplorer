Data Sources

Data from the EPA National Rivers and Streams Assessment 2018–19 (DOI: 10.23719/1528396). Probability-based national survey covering randomized stream sites across Colorado, Idaho, Utah, Washington, and Wyoming.

Density = trout observed / electrofished reach length (fish per km). Wetted width was not recorded in NRSA surveys, so fish-per-area density cannot be calculated.
