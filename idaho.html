Data Sources

Data from the Idaho Department of Fish and Game Standard Stream Survey (IDFG SSS). Over 1.2 million individual fish records from electrofishing and snorkeling surveys across Idaho, 1969–2025.

Reach dimensions (length × width) were not included in this export, so fish-per-area density cannot be calculated. Avg fish per visit is a relative abundance index only.
