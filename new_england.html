Data Sources

Data compiled from NH Fish & Game, CT DEEP, USGS Conte Lab, and VT Fish & Wildlife. Electrofishing survey data spans 1983–2025 from 4 agencies across 2,719 streams in NH, CT, MA & VT.

Density = trout observed / stream area sampled (per 100m²). Estimated (~) densities use assumed 100m reach lengths and average widths where area data not available. Maine data pending.
